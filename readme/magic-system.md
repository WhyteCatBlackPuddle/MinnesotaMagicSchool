# Magic System

Magic is both mysterious and understandable. Magic has rules, but also appears naturally without manipulation.

## Who can do it?

Anyone that has magical affinity.

Some humans are born with innate ability through blood.

Some acquire it through contact with magical places, objects or other mysterious ways.

However: Magic by humans requires discipline, knowledge and skilled mentors.

## How does it first appear in children?

It appears during moments of high stress or vulnerability.

Most have access to their magical affinity by age 12 but not all do.

## Affinity and education

Students arrive with raw or accidental magical affinity. The school teaches discipline, tools, theory, ethics, and practical spellwork so they can use magic safely and responsibly.

Affinity is what magic does around you before you are trained. Education is what teaches you what to do with it.

## What can first-years do?

Very little because they are un-disciplined. Magic by humans requires discipline, knowledge and skilled mentors.

## What can older students do?

Perform spells with wands and other magic implements.

Read ancient texts.

Protect themselves from evil.

Do good.

Write Runes that influence present objects through enchantment.

## What is dangerous?

People can be killed trying to use it.
