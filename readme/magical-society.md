# Magical Society

Boundary Waters Academy is a boarding school for magically-affined children, located in the north woods of Minnesota. Its setting is remote and nature-rich, but its curriculum prepares students for life in the wider magical world: cities, professions, households, governments, shops, hospitals, research, craft, defense, and community life.

## Core world assumptions

- broader magical society exists
- urban magical centers exist
- practical magical jobs exist
- Boundary Waters is one academy among others
- its local specialty is teaching amid strong wild magic
- students are beginners with affinities, not heroes with powers
- magic is teachable but never fully tame

## What makes Boundary Waters magic unique?

The Boundary Waters academy magic is rooted in nature, but also focused on practical everyday modern Magic in the magic community in urban centers. Like most early life training its related to what Magicians would practically do.
