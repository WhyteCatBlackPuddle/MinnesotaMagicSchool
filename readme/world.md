# Boundary Waters Academy World Foundation

Boundary Waters Academy is a boarding school for magically-affined children, located in the north woods of Minnesota. Its setting is remote and nature-rich, but its curriculum prepares students for life in the wider magical world: cities, professions, households, governments, shops, hospitals, research, craft, defense, and community life. Students arrive with raw or accidental magical affinity. The school teaches discipline, tools, theory, ethics, and practical spellwork so they can use magic safely and responsibly.

Affinity is what magic does around you before you are trained. Education is what teaches you what to do with it.

## Core world assumptions

- broader magical society exists
- urban magical centers exist
- practical magical jobs exist
- Boundary Waters is one academy among others
- its local specialty is teaching amid strong wild magic
- students are beginners with affinities, not heroes with powers
- magic is teachable but never fully tame
