# School Curriculum

Boundary Waters Academy is a boarding school for magically-affined children, located in the north woods of Minnesota. Its setting is remote and nature-rich, but its curriculum prepares students for life in the wider magical world: cities, professions, households, governments, shops, hospitals, research, craft, defense, and community life.

## First-year core classes

- Introductory Spellwork
- Wand and Implement Safety
- Runes and Symbolic Enchantment
- Magical Materials and Potions
- Wards, Boundaries, and Protection
- Magical History and Society
- Ethics of Human Magic
- Field Practicum
- Affinity Observation

## Later-year courses

- Defensive Spellwork
- Advanced Enchantment
- Practical Household Magic
- Urban Magical Systems
- Magical Craft and Repair
- Healing Foundations
- Translation of Ancient Texts
- Magical Law and Civic Life
- Creature Handling
- Transportation and Communication Magic
- Professional Apprenticeship
- Environmental and Place-Based Magic
