A campus of lodges and workshops on a lake in the Minnesota Boundary Waters — not on any map, not reachable by road. Three hundred students arrive each autumn, joining the two hundred or so people who live here year-round. The village and the school grew up together; nobody agrees which came first. The lake is old, the pines are older, and everyone here is learning something.
